### District Revenue
It is found that all the military bases have some sub-divisions where the the revenue is generated from the slot machines. This file does contain messy data and is divided into 3 different formats, out of which 2nd and 3rd format have been cleaned and updated. Merging it with Asset Reports and Financial Statements could help in predicting and identifying machine installation and revenue generation trends.

### Financial Statements
This is the largest file among all. Financial Statements, contains tables having monthly records for all the regions and bases, tracking the cash flow history from the year 2020 to year 2024. Moreover, there are 4 major table formats present in Financial Statements. Let's understand them:-

#### Format-1 (Overall Summary of Cash Flow in Assets, Liabilities and Equity)
This format gives us an overview on how has the cash flown in the Assets(Slot Machines) and in the Liabilities(Maintenance and Salaries of People). This table repeats in the beginning of every data entry cycle, every month. Providing enough scope for a brief analysis that how much cash flow has happened per region and totally by the US Government.

#### Format-2 (Actual vs Budget)
This format contains the Budget of different fields per region, all over the world and the actual amount of money spent on assets and liabilities. Format 2 contains important fields such as :-

- Gaming Machine Revenue
- Direct NAFI Reimbursement Gaming NAFI
- Net Revenue
- Operating Expenses
- Distributions
- Net Income

Also, you can find 3 tables of this format in one single data entry cycle. These tables have details of the same major field, but for 3 different major regions which are _Europe_, _Korea_ and _Japan_.

_One can perform analysis on trends in the budget planned by the government and the actual cash flow._

#### Format-3 (Analysis per Military Type)
Format-3 is similar to Format-2 in terms of the important data fields, since both have same fields. But the main difference between these 2 is that, Format-2 focuses on comparison  between Actual Cash FLow and the Budget that was set up, but Format-3 focuses mainly on how much investment is made on which military branch, from:-

- Army
- Navy
- Marines

_One can analyze the trends in usage of cash per military branch, and by mixing it with Format-2, one can make a hypothesis that which specific military body is using excess amount of government funds and in which region._

#### Format-4 (Revenue Generation per Branch)
This format suggests that how much revenue was generated by which specific branch of military per month. An analysis on branches has been made that which are the Top 10 branches generating maximum revenue. A time-series analysis has also been performed using this format, predicting revenue generation trends in upcoming years.

### Marine Revenue
Marine Revenue basically has 2 formats, one in which an average summary is maintained and another one contains detailed revenue generations per month and per base. 

- For the Format-1, a pattern was monitored that this format contains total revenue generation of the current year along with previous 4 years. This PDF contains data collection from the year 2020 to 2024, and based on the justified data collections trend, we have total revenue generation figures from the year 2016 up to the year 2024 for all the Marine Bases.
- For Format-2, it is observed that there there was data collection every month for every Marine Base but sometimes no data was uploaded, hence leaving negligible revenue figures. It could also be observed that the year total was measured in the end of September Month.
